About Eco-Calc


What is Eco-Calc?


With Eco-Calc, you can learn how to how to make more sustainable and environmentally-friendly choices. Based off your current electricity and/or water usage, recommendations that will benefit the environment will be personalized for YOU.

Created to address SDG's #7 (affordable and clean energy), 8 (decent work and economic growth), 9 (industry, innovation, and infrastructure), 11 (sustainable cities and communities), 12 (responsible consumption and production), 13 (climate action), 14 (life below water), and 15 (life on land)

Not only does Eco-Calc offer solutions to businesses and personal households to reduce their water input, fostering economically friendly alternatives, but it promotes the reduction of excessive water and electricity usage, two areas which negatively harm the environment such as through carbon emissions.
